How it works
The person you're looking for
is already looking for you.

Branches walks you through a real dating experience — not an endless scroll. Four stages. Real intention. And a connection that starts where it matters most.

1

Your voice goes first

Record a short voice intro — who you are, what matters to you, what you're looking for. No photos. No filters. Just you. Browse others the same way and invite someone whose voice stays with you into a private Pod.

2

Get to know them — really

Inside your Pod, it's just the two of you. Talk through voice notes, text, or scheduled virtual dates. Send a gift if they make you smile. There's no rush and no distractions — just space to find out if this is something real.

3

The reveal. Then the real date.

When you're both ready, photos are shared. If it feels right, plan your first in-person date right in the app — with built-in safety features so you can focus on the moment, not the worry.

4

Let it grow

Your relationship has a home here. As things progress — from talking to dating to something more — your status updates with you. And when you're ready to go off the market, we'll celebrate that too.
